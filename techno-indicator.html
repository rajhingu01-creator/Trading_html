Shreeji techno indicator

Shreeji Techno Indicator is a versatile, multi-market technical analysis tool designed to assist traders in identifying potential market trends across Indian indices, Forex, and Cryptocurrency markets.

The indicator is specifically developed to help capture strong market movements (rallies) and trend opportunities by combining price action, momentum analysis, and trend-following techniques. It is structured to highlight possible entry zones during trending conditions while filtering out low-probability signals.

It supports multiple timeframes, making it suitable for both scalping (short-term trades) and swing trading (medium-term positions).

Based on historical observations, the indicator demonstrates an approximate accuracy range of 70–75% under normal market conditions, though performance may vary depending on market volatility and external factors.

This tool is intended strictly for analytical and educational purposes only.
